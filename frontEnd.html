What is reCAPTCHA?

reCAPTCHA is an anti-robot verification service developed by Google. It is claimed to have advantages of advanced security, ease of Use and creation of value. It uses an advanced risk analysis engine and adaptive CAPTCHAs to prevent automated software from engaging in abusive activities on the webSite.

How reCAPTCHA works?

After clicking the check box next to "I'm not a robot", it will pop up a small window containing some images as well as a related question. Users need to follow the instruction inside the question and choose the correct images. If the answer is correct, reCAPTCHA will verify that you are a human instead of a robot; otherwise, a new set of images and a new question will be regenerated and let the users try it again.
